BLOCK-LEVEL-ELEMENTS:

<div>: Division or container for grouping content.
<p>: Paragraph.
<h1>, <h2>, ..., <h6>: Headings of different levels.
<ul>: Unordered list.
<ol>: Ordered list.
<li>: List item.
<table>: Table.
<form>: Form container.
Block-level elements typically start on a new line and take up the full width of their container.

INLINE-LEVEL-ELEMENTS:
<span>: Inline container for grouping text or other inline elements.
<a>: Anchor for creating hyperlinks.
<strong> or <b>: Bold text.
<em> or <i>: Italicized text.
<img>: Image.
<br>: Line break.
<input>: Input field.
<button>: Button.
